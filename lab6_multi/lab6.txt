Story Premise

You are a help desk engineer.  Tickets begin coming in from a corporate office where strange things are happening:

badge readers fail
    users hear groaning in the server room
    someone in Facilities hasn’t blinked in 20 minutes
    coffee machines are behaving in deeply suspicious ways
    sysadmins are moving slowly but with great determination

It may be:
    malware
    a facilities problem
    a biological incident
    or, unfortunately, zombies

🧱 Architecture
LLM 1 — Story Generator

This LLM writes the narrative scene.

It handles:
    mood
    humor
    creepy events
    office absurdity

LLM 2 — Game Master

This LLM reads the scene and outputs structured game state.

It decides:
    threat level
    whether the office is safe
    whether security/facilities/IR should be called
    what the next choices are
